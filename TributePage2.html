About Kimi ni Todoke (From Me To You)   Genre: Romance

  Kuronuma Sawako has always been feared by her peers. She's a quiet and kind girl who gets mistaken for a ghost like figure, earning herself the name of Sadako, who is a vengeful ghost of a physic in Japanese folklore. However, Kuronuma's reputation turns around when she meets a generous boy named Kazehaya. Their friendship blossoms into love as they both learn to speak up for themselves and others. The love doesn't stop as Kuronuma's newfound friends, Yoshida and Ayane, each find a unique love of their own.
